How TopFarms works

From Facebook post to filled role — without the chaos

TopFarms brings together job listings and candidate profiles from across New Zealand's agricultural sector, then layers AI matching on top so both sides find the right fit, fast.

01
👤
Build your profile
Tell us your experience, shed types, preferred regions, and living situation. Takes 5 minutes — or upload your CV and we'll parse it for you.
02
🎯
Get matched
Our AI scores your profile against every listing — rotary experience matches rotary farms, accommodation needs match what's on offer. No noise.
03
📋
Apply in one tap
Your profile is your application. Employers see your experience, qualifications, and match score instantly — no covering letter needed unless you want to add one.
04
🤝
Get hired
Track your applications, receive interview requests, and manage offers — all in your dashboard. TopFarms candidates average 12 days from apply to offer.
