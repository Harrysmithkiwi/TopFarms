📋 Role overview

We're looking for a reliable, enthusiastic dairy farm assistant to join our tight-knit team on our family-owned rotary operation outside Te Awamutu. This is a permanent, full-time role with genuine opportunity for growth — we back our people and support ongoing training through DairyNZ.

You'll be working alongside the farm owner and one other assistant, taking real ownership of your responsibilities from day one. The role suits someone who takes pride in animal care, wants to develop their skills, and is looking for a place to settle in for the long term — not just a season.

🌅 Day-to-day responsibilities
Twice-daily milking on a 50-bail rotary shed (AM and PM rotations, shared with team)
Herd health monitoring — identifying and reporting any animal health concerns to the farm owner
Calf rearing during calving season (spring, August–October)
General farm maintenance — fencing, laneways, water troughs and infrastructure upkeep
Tractor operation for feed-out and pasture management tasks
Assisting with pasture management and farm record keeping
On-call responsibilities on a shared roster (typically 1 in 3 weekends)

🌿 About Greenfield Farms

Family-owned dairy operation, been in the family 25 years. We milk 520 Friesian-cross cows on a 210-hectare property near Te Awamutu. We believe in work-life balance and looking after our team — you'll be trusted to manage your responsibilities without being micromanaged.

We've had low staff turnover — our last assistant stayed nearly four years. We're a tight-knit team of three and we genuinely enjoy what we do. If you're looking for somewhere to grow your skills and put down some roots, we'd like to meet you.
