# TopFarms GTM — Weekly Operating Rhythm

*2026-07-02 · Built on the Playbook's founder weekly cadence. Ties the funnel + Asana + Linear together. Solo, ~60 hr/wk, handoff-ready.*

## Hard timing rules (non-negotiable)

- **Never DM, post, or call during milking: 4–7am and 3–6pm.** Post/call windows: ~10am–2pm and evening after 7pm.
- **FB cold DMs: ~20–40/day to non-connections, ramp from ~10–15/day in week 1.** This is the **combined ceiling across employer + seeker DMs on the one account**, not per-audience — the ~30/day seeker supplement must fit *under* it alongside any employer FB DMs (e.g. 25 seeker + 10 employer = 35, within cap). Protect the account (it admins the group). Low volume, warm account. This is a supplement, not the seeker engine.
- **Employer outbound: ~10 touches/week** is the Playbook target. Quality over quantity.

## The week (Playbook cadence, adapted)

| Block | Activity | Hrs | System touchpoints |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon AM** | Target-list review + cold-call session (Tier A) | 2.5 | Asana Employer Pipeline: pull Raw target cards; log calls as comments; move to Contacted |
| **Tue (alt weeks)** | Farm-visit day — 2–3 farms, one region | 8 | Asana: move Qualified/Onboarded; notes = war stories |
| **Wed** | Cold email batch + calls + demos | 5 | Supabase Outreach queue: approve + send drafted; Asana: schedule follow-ups |
| **Thu** | Follow-up sweep (touch 2/3/4) + onboarding calls | 3 | Asana: cadence position field; new employer → Founding cohort |
| **Fri** | Weekly review + field presence (saleyard / Farmlands) | 3 | `metrics-dashboard.md` numbers; Asana content calendar review |

## Threaded through the week (not blocks)

- **FB group (owned) — the seeker engine.** 2× value posts/week + keep the pinned "make a free profile, workers never pay" post fresh. This does the seeker heavy-lifting, not DMs. Post in the 10am–2pm window.
- **Content calendar (Asana).** Batch one theme's assets per week (Playbook 4-week cycle: We exist → What we do → It works → Join us). Carousels via `topfarms-carousel`.
- **Cold seeker DMs (~30/day supplement).** Slot into non-milking windows; target "looking for work" posters; plain-text first touch, no link (see `outreach-templates.md`).
- **LinkedIn founder posts** 2×/week (workforce-thinker, not vendor).

## Engineering (Linear) — kept out of the GTM week

Eng is quiescent. A single **Fri 30-min** slot to triage Linear (the three quality gates + any new bug). Do not let eng bleed into sales blocks; do not let GTM tasks land in Linear.

## The one discipline that carries everything

The Playbook's line, kept verbatim as the weekly self-check: **is the founder on the phone / in the truck 60% of the time, laptop 40%?** If a week drifts laptop-heavy, the funnel stalls. Founder time is the bottleneck, not tools.
