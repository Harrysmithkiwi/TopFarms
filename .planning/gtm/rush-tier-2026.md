# Rush tier 2026: the calving wedge (relief milkers + calf rearers)

*2026-07-08 · LOCKED #10: it is July, calving is starting, the autumn wave is gone. The wedge NOW is urgent, small-ticket, fast placements: relief milkers and calf rearers. Full demos and the sit-down Founding-25 conversation get booked for October. This campaign is how TopFarms is useful to a farmer THIS month instead of asking for attention they do not have. Voice per `docs/_canonical/TopFarms_Outreach_Reply_Config.md`. Timing rules per `weekly-operating-rhythm.md`: never post, DM or call 4–7am / 3–6pm.*

## Target

Farms that need a relief milker or calf rearer **now**, dairy regions, calving underway or days away. The buyer state is "short-handed this week", not "rethinking how I hire". Sell speed and fit, small ticket, guarantee-backed.

## Sources

1. **The leads pipeline (Supabase).** Filter `leads` for relief milker / calf rearer / farm assistant roles posted in the last 14 days. These jump the Tier queue: a calving-season relief post is Tier A by urgency regardless of farm size. Lane B gets the drafted first message (money-free, per config); Lane A / warm replies go straight to the phone.
2. **The owned FB group.** Group posts (drafts below) in the 10am–2pm window. Zero ban-risk, this is the volume channel.
3. **Sales-kit pivots.** Every "too busy, it's calving" objection on any call routes here (see `segment-a-sales-kit.md`).

## Offer framing (fast, small, guarantee-backed)

- **Fast:** relief and calf-rearing listings are short and concrete (dates, shed, roster). Matching is quickest where the must-haves are simplest. The pitch is days, not weeks. Do not promise a number of days we have not hit yet; say "quick" and then be quick.
- **Small:** first listing free. Placement fee $350 [lab: test $300/$400] on hire only, Net-14. Small against a missed milking roster, and we let THEM do that maths on the phone, we do not lecture it.
- **Guarantee-backed:** 3 qualified applicants or you don't pay (exact terms in `founding-25-offer.md`, quote the plain phone version).
- **Money placement:** dollar figures live on the phone and the signup page. Group posts may carry the guarantee in plain words but **no dollar figures**. The cold DM carries **no money at all**, not the fee, not "free", not the guarantee (config hard rule). Rush placements are full Segment-A pricing; the [MODELLED] ~$150 casual fee in PRICING-MODEL §8 is dormant spec, not live, do not quote it (see tension note at the bottom).

**Variation notes (mandatory, config variation kit):** these are registers to write from, not copy to repeat. Rotate openers ("Calving's kicking off" / "Right, calving's here" / "Week one of calving down our way"), rotate which role leads (relief milker vs calf rearer), rotate sign-off (bare Harry / none / "good luck this season"), and re-anchor each post in that week's reality (weather, region, a comment thread from the group). Two value posts a week is the ceiling (weekly rhythm); rush posts count toward it, they do not stack on top.

## Success metric: placements, not signups

The rush tier is measured on **verified placements** (north-star event: application → `hired`, `placement_fees.confirmed_at` set). A pile of rush listings with no fills is a failed campaign and a supply problem, not a marketing win. Watch weekly, per `metrics-dashboard.md` Friday review:

1. Rush listings posted (relief milker + calf rearer).
2. Qualified applicants per rush listing (guarantee threshold is 3, this is also the guarantee-trigger early warning: PRICING-MODEL says fix supply, not price, if makegoods run >15%).
3. **Rush placements confirmed** (the number that counts).
4. Time-to-placement on rush roles (this is the "fast" claim being earned or not).
