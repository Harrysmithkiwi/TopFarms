Why TopFarms

We match on what actually matters on-farm

A generic job board sees a title and a region. TopFarms reads the texture of farm work, so workers find roles that fit their life and employers hire people who stay.

Accommodation

On-farm house, single quarters, or bring-your-own. We match what is offered to what is needed.

Couples & family

Two-role placements and family-friendly farms, surfaced together rather than as an afterthought.

Shed & system

Rotary or herringbone, robotic or conventional. The detail that decides whether a worker is a fit.

Livestock & sector

Dairy, sheep & beef, and the wider sector, with the stock experience each role actually calls for.

Region & travel

Distance, relocation, and the realities of rural life weighed into every score, not bolted on.

Experience & tickets

Years on the land, qualifications, and the practical tickets that prove someone can do the work.
