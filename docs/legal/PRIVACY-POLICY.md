TopFarms is a New Zealand agricultural job marketplace at topfarms.co.nz. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why, who we share it with, and your rights under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. We have written it in plain English on purpose.

---

## 1. What we collect

### If you are a job seeker (worker)

- **Account details:** name, email address, password (stored hashed by our auth provider).
- **Profile details:** skills, experience, work preferences, location, and your visa or residency status. Visa status is stored as a category only (NZ citizen, permanent resident, working holiday, student, or needs sponsorship) — we do not store visa numbers or immigration case details.
- **Contact details:** phone number and email held in a separate, protected record (see section 4 on how these are released).
- **Documents you upload:** CVs, certificates, references, and identity documents. All are stored in a private storage bucket that is not publicly accessible.

### If you are an employer

- **Account details:** name, email address, password (stored hashed).
- **Business details:** farm or business name, location, verification documents and farm photos you provide, job listings you post.
- **Payment details:** handled by Stripe, our payment processor. We never see or store your full card number.

### Everyone

- **Technical basics:** the authentication session cookie needed to keep you signed in, and standard server logs (IP address, timestamps) generated by our hosting providers.

## 2. Why we collect it

- To run the marketplace: matching workers with farm jobs, showing employers relevant candidates, and letting you apply for roles.
- To verify identity and eligibility to work, which helps keep the platform trustworthy for both sides.
- To generate AI-assisted match explanations and candidate summaries (see section 3).
- To process payments (employer listing fees and placement fees).
- To send transactional email (application updates, verification outcomes, invoices).
- To meet our legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information. We do not use your information for third-party advertising.

## 3. AI processing — what does and does not reach the model

We use Claude, an AI model operated by Anthropic (a US company), to generate match explanations and candidate summaries.

**What is sent to the AI:** profile information such as skills, experience, work preferences, and your categorical visa status (for example "working holiday" or "needs sponsorship"). Visa status is included because eligibility to work is a core part of whether a match is realistic, and we want to be upfront that this category crosses to a US-based provider.

**What is never sent to the AI:** your identity documents, your contact details, and the contents of uploaded files. Identity documents are excluded from AI processing entirely.

## 4. The contact-release mechanic (how employers get your details)

Being honest about how this works: your phone number and email are held in a protected record that employers cannot see by default. An employer only receives your contact details after they acknowledge TopFarms' placement fee for hiring you. This is enforced at the database level, not just in the interface. Until that acknowledgement happens, employers see your profile and application but not your direct contact details.

## 5. Identity documents — extra protections

Identity documents get the strictest handling on the platform:

- They are **never shown to employers**. This is enforced at three independent layers: a database access policy, a server-side allowlist in the function that issues document links, and a filter in the interface.
- Only TopFarms administrators can view them, via links that expire after 15 minutes.
- They are never sent to any AI model.

## 7. How long we keep information

**Marketplace data (your account and profile):** we keep it while your account is active. Scheduled deletion timelines for closed accounts (a 90-day window after seeker account deletion, and 12 months of inactivity for employer data) have been scoped but are **[PENDING] — not yet implemented**. Today, if you want your account and data deleted, email hello@topfarms.co.nz and we will do it manually.

**Leads pipeline (public job posts we collect for admin outreach — not marketplace data):**

- Collected posts are staged and reviewed by a human before anything is kept.
- Rejected or stale staged items are purged after 30 days (automated weekly).
- Dead leads have personal information stripped after 6 months (automated monthly).
- If you ask us to stop contacting you, your details go on a suppression list that is honoured permanently — we keep the minimum needed to make sure we never contact you again. Email hello@topfarms.co.nz.

## 8. The leads pipeline (for people we contact who are not users)

TopFarms staff collect publicly posted farm job listings (for example from public job boards and public social posts) to invite farm employers to the platform. This is admin-only tooling, separate from marketplace data. Every collected post is reviewed by a human before retention, retention is limited as set out in section 7, and opting out is permanent. If we have contacted you and you would rather we did not, email hello@topfarms.co.nz.

## 9. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you can:

- **Ask for a copy** of the personal information we hold about you.
- **Ask us to correct it** if it is wrong.

Email hello@topfarms.co.nz and we will respond within 20 working days as the Act requires.

If you are not happy with how we have handled your information or your request, you can complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner: **privacy.org.nz** | 0800 803 909.

## 10. If something goes wrong (data breaches)

If a privacy breach occurs that is likely to cause serious harm, we will notify the Office of the Privacy Commissioner and affected people as soon as practicable, as the Privacy Act 2020 requires. Our internal breach response process is documented and tested against this commitment.

## 11. Cookies

We keep it minimal: TopFarms uses the cookie/session storage needed to keep you signed in (set by our authentication provider). We do not run third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

## 12. Security

Access to data is restricted by row-level database policies (each user can only reach their own records), documents live in private storage buckets, admin access to sensitive documents uses short-lived links, and administrative actions are recorded in an audit log.

## 13. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that matters, we will post the updated version at topfarms.co.nz/privacy with a new "last updated" date, and for significant changes we will email account holders.
