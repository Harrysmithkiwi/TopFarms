# TopFarms — Partnership Battlecards (NZ · AU · UK)

> **STATUS: FORWARD STRATEGY — activates on EXPANSION-MODEL triggers; nothing here is live.**
> Created 2026-07-08 (Stage-2 remediation, expansion workstream). One card per named body.
> **Honest status on every card: NOT APPROACHED — future.** No relationship exists with
> any organisation below; nothing here implies otherwise. All org names/roles [V]-checked
> July 2026 (sources footnoted). What-we-offer / first-ask lines are [OPINION] unless
> labelled. Founder owns all first conversations (AU-PLAYBOOK §5 division of labour).

**The common shape of every card:** they have members/levy-payers and credibility; we
have (eventually) the Index dataset, workforce insights, and a workers-never-pay
marketplace their members can use. We want distribution and credibility, not money.
**The common risk on every card:** a body deciding to build or badge its own jobs board —
several already run one. That risk is listed per card, not hand-waved.

---

## NZ

### DairyNZ
- **What:** industry-good body for NZ dairy, levy-funded; runs the **GoDairy** careers/
  attraction programme (~$1.24M invested in "attract" work) [V — dairynz.co.nz, godairy.co.nz].
- **We offer:** live hiring-demand and wage-band data (Index-NZ) for their attraction and
  advocacy work; a destination for GoDairy-generated candidates that never charges them.
- **We want:** GoDairy → TopFarms referral pathway; presence in their channels; data
  partnership credibility ("workforce insights in partnership with…" — their sign-off, earned).
- **Realistic first ask:** one meeting with the GoDairy/people team; a data-sharing memo
  on regional vacancy trends. Not a logo swap on day one.
- **Risk:** DairyNZ is a co-sponsor of the Farm Source jobs board lineage (Fencepost was
  a Fonterra+DairyNZ project [V — scoop.co.nz 2010]) — the "build/badge their own board"
  risk is not hypothetical here; it already half-exists.
- **Status: not approached — future.**

### Federated Farmers
- **What:** membership-based advocacy organisation for NZ farmers; works alongside
  DairyNZ/B+LNZ on shared issues [V — dairynz.co.nz partnerships page].
- **We offer:** member value (a hiring tool that isn't a recruiter), workforce-shortage
  evidence for their advocacy (Index data beats anecdote in a select-committee submission).
- **We want:** member-comms distribution (newsletter/regional meetings), credibility with
  the exact owner-operator ICP.
- **Realistic first ask:** a regional-president intro in a live TopFarms region + one
  piece of Index data for their employment advocacy.
- **Risk:** low build-risk (advocacy body, not a product shop); real risk is neutrality —
  they back members, not vendors, so expect "useful, but no endorsement" for a long time.
- **Status: not approached — future.**

### Beef + Lamb New Zealand
- **What:** levy-funded industry body for sheep and beef farmers; collaborates with
  DairyNZ and Federated Farmers to avoid duplication [V — beeflambnz.com].
- **We offer:** the same Index/insights play, timed to the **drystock vertical switch-on**
  (EXPANSION-MODEL §2, vertical #2 — lambing/shearing wedges), not before.
- **We want:** credibility outside dairy at the exact moment we expand there; field-day
  and extension-programme presence.
- **Realistic first ask:** nothing until vertical #2 is live — then a shearing/lambing
  seasonal-labour data snapshot as the door-opener. Approaching pre-vertical would be
  selling a board with no sheep jobs on it.
- **Risk:** thin hiring frequency per farm in drystock could make us look shallow if we
  approach early (the real risk is us, not them).
- **Status: not approached — future (gated on drystock vertical).**

### NZ Young Farmers
- **What:** national membership organisation for young people in food & fibre; runs the
  **FMG Young Farmer of the Year** contest series [V — youngfarmers.co.nz; 2026 final held
  in Taranaki, won by Tom Slee].
- **We offer:** free profiles and career pathway visibility to exactly the seeker side we
  want; possible contest/regional-event presence later.
- **We want:** seeker-side distribution and youth credibility — the one NZ card that is
  supply-side first.
- **Realistic first ask:** a "getting your first farm job" content collab with one region.
- **Risk:** sponsor-crowded space (FMG, Ravensdown, et al. [V]) — we are not a sponsor-fee
  payer; if the relationship is only purchasable, park it.
- **Status: not approached — future.**

### Rural banks & insurers (as Index BUYERS, not distribution)
- **Named candidates [V — all active in NZ rural sector]:** Rabobank NZ, FMG (rural
  insurer; title sponsor of Young Farmer of the Year), ANZ/BNZ/Westpac rural divisions.
- **What they are to us:** the first commercial customers for **Index/data licensing**
  (PRICING §3 future line) — rural lenders and insurers price risk on labour availability
  and farm operational data they currently can't see.
- **We offer:** regional workforce liquidity/wage/vacancy indices — data nobody else has
  if the marketplace wins.
- **We want:** revenue (licence fees), not distribution. This is the one card where money
  is the ask.
- **Realistic first ask:** nothing until the Index has ≥12 months of NZ data depth
  [OPINION]; then one pilot report to one bank's rural economics team.
- **Risk:** they have in-house economics teams and survey lines; the Index must show flow
  data (real listings/placements) they cannot replicate — otherwise it's a nice PDF.
- **Status: not approached — future (gated on Index maturity).**

## AU (activate at AU entry, not before)

### Dairy Australia
- **What:** national services body for the AU dairy industry (levy-funded); runs a **free
  Dairy Farm Jobs Board** and a national jobs-attraction campaign [V — dairyaustralia.com.au;
  dairy.com.au/careers/find-a-job, live July 2026].
- **We offer:** matching, trust/verification, and roster/accommodation depth their free
  listing board doesn't attempt; Index-AU regional workforce data for their people/
  attraction programmes.
- **We want:** coexistence-to-referral — their campaign generates jobseeker attention;
  TopFarms converts it into matches. Their credibility with exactly our beachhead
  (Gippsland dairy).
- **Realistic first ask:** a data conversation with their people/workforce team; cross-
  listing or referral pilot in one region.
- **Risk:** **highest own-board risk on this list** — the board already exists and is
  free. Counter: it's a classifieds surface, not a matching/trust layer; our pitch must
  be complement-not-replacement or it dies in the room. [OPINION]
- **Status: not approached — future (gated on AU entry).**

### National Farmers' Federation (NFF)
- **What:** national peak body for AU agriculture (est. 1979), federation of state
  organisations and commodity councils [V — nff.org.au]. **Currency note [V — Grain
  Central/Sheep Central 2026]:** membership in flux — NSW Farmers gave notice to resign;
  VFF stayed on reduced fees citing financial hardship.
- **We offer:** national workforce-shortage data for their advocacy (labour is a
  perennial NFF policy front).
- **We want:** national-level credibility umbrella once multi-state.
- **Realistic first ask:** none at entry — **go state-level first** (below); NFF is a
  year-2+ conversation and its current instability lowers its practical value [OPINION].
- **Risk:** peak-body flux means a deal here may not reach actual farmers.
- **Status: not approached — future.**

### State farming organisations (the ones that matter for the beachhead)
- **Victorian Farmers Federation (VFF)** — Victorian state body, est. 1979 [V — nff.org.au
  members; energybriefing.org.au]. Beachhead-state priority #1; dairy sits under its
  commodity structure. First ask: regional meeting presence + member-comms mention in
  Gippsland.
- **NSW Farmers** — NSW association [V]; relevant at Riverina expansion, not entry.
- **AgForce Queensland** — Queensland producers' body (beef/sheep/grain/cane) [V —
  nff.org.au members]; relevant only when the hort/QLD vertical opens.
- Same offer/want/risk shape as Federated Farmers NZ (advocacy bodies, low build-risk,
  slow endorsement). **Status all: not approached — future.**

## UK (activate at UK entry, not before)

### NFU (National Farmers' Union)
- **What:** membership union for farmers/growers in England & Wales; actively publishes
  Seasonal Worker scheme guidance for growers [V — nfuonline.com]. (NFU Scotland and NFU
  Cymru/Ulster Farmers' Union are related-but-separate bodies — treat separately at
  regional expansion [A].)
- **We offer:** workforce data for their labour-policy advocacy (they are the loudest
  voice on seasonal/permanent ag labour shortages); member hiring value.
- **We want:** credibility with estates and family farms; the "Yorkshire farmer" trust
  transfer.
- **Realistic first ask:** one Index-UK data briefing to their horticulture/labour policy
  team once ≥2 anchor accounts are live (data first, membership channel later).
- **Risk:** NFU is courted by every agri-startup; without live UK traction we're noise.
  Hence anchors-before-NFU sequencing. [OPINION]
- **Status: not approached — future.**

### AHDB (Agriculture and Horticulture Development Board)
- **What:** statutory levy board funded by farmers/growers, not government; current
  active sectors: beef & lamb, dairy, cereals & oilseeds, pork. **The horticulture and
  potato levies ended** (post-2021 levy-payer votes) — AHDB now only hosts legacy hort
  content [V — ahdb.org.uk funding-and-structure; horticulture.ahdb.org.uk].
- **We offer:** placement-flow and wage data that complements their market-intelligence
  remit; a data partnership, not a membership play (levy body ≠ member channel).
- **We want:** analytical credibility for Index-UK (AHDB citation/collaboration is the
  UK equivalent of a DairyNZ data sign-off).
- **Realistic first ask:** a methodology conversation with their market-intelligence/
  economics team — deliberately unglamorous.
- **Risk:** as a statutory body it will not endorse a commercial platform; the ceiling is
  "data collaborator", and note the hort-levy gap means AHDB is NOT a channel to
  horticulture at all. Scheme operators fill that hole (UK-PLAYBOOK §2).
- **Status: not approached — future.**
